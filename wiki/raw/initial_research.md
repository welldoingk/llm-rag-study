# Latest RAG Trends Research (2024-2025)

## Summary
The RAG landscape in 2024–2025 has shifted from basic, experimental "naive RAG" implementations to sophisticated, production-ready "Context Engines."

## Key Topics
1. **Agentic RAG**: Transition from reactive to proactive systems. Autonomy, reasoned planning, and multi-step execution.
2. **GraphRAG**: Structural rigor using Knowledge Graphs. Better for global queries and complex reasoning.
3. **Hybrid Search**: Combining Dense (Vector) and Sparse (BM25) search for better precision and recall.
4. **Long-Context LLMs**: Complementary to RAG. RAG handles dynamic/massive data, while long-context handles deep reasoning over retrieved info.
5. **Evaluation & Multimodality**: Emergence of LLM-based evaluation and integration of non-text data.
